Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a variety of toppings on a flatbread base.

It is quick to prepare and can be customized to suit different tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 3 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup hickory flavored barbeque sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon molasses
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ½ bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 cup smoked Gouda cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup thinly sliced red onion

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small saucepan, combine barbeque sauce, honey, molasses, and brown sugar. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in chopped cilantro.
  3. Spread the barbeque sauce mixture evenly over the pre-baked pizza crust.
  4. Top with cooked chicken, shredded Gouda cheese, and sliced red onion.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
