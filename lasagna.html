Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

It takes some time to prepare, but the result is well worth it.

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 4 (15oz) cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (15oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook ground beef and sausage in large pot over medium heat until browned; drain. Stir in tomato sauce, mushrooms, garlic salt, oregano, thyme, and basil; season with salt and black pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles with olive oil in the boiling water, stirring occasionally. Cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  4. Combine ricotta, eggs, and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  5. Cover bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish with a layer of meat sauce; layer 3 noodles on top. Spread about 1/3 ricotta cheese mixture over noodles; sprinkle with 1/4 mozzarella cheese over the top.Spoon about 1/3 meat sauce over mozzarella cheese. Repeat layering twice more, finishing with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese melts and sauce bubbles , about 90 minutes. Cover lasagna with foil if cheese begins to get too dark. Let sit before serving for 10 to 15 minutes.
