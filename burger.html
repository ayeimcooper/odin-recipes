Burger

A classic burger is a simple yet delicious sandwich made with a juicy patty, fresh vegetables, and your favorite condiments.

Ingredients:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 hamburger patties
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 leaves of lettuce
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 4 slices of onion
  • 4 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons of ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons of mustard

Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the hamburger patties on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  3. While the patties are cooking, prepare the buns by toasting them lightly.
  4. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on each bun, followed by cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  5. Drizzle with mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard to taste.
